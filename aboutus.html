About Us

Our History

Started in 2013, Restaurant conFusion quickly established itself as a culinary icon for excellence in India. With its unique brand of world fusion cuisine that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list client in India. Featuring four of the best Michelin chefs with Sanjeev Kapoor in the world, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to The Frying Pan, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Aakash Kumar, that featured for the first time the world's best cuisines in a pan.
